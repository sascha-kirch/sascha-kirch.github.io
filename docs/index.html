About Me

AI/ML Engineer | Multi-Modal Perception & Generative AI | Foundation Models

I build deep learning systems for multi-sensor perception and generative AI. Author of 24+ technical articles on foundation models, attention optimization, and state space architectures.

My career spans hardware design (FPGA, high-speed digital systems, automotive radar deployed in 100M+ vehicles) to leading engineering teams to AI expert. I think in terms of latency, memory bandwidth, and real-time constraints — connecting cutting-edge AI with the hardware it runs on.

At Bosch, I develop perception systems processing multi-sensor data for autonomous driving. I work with foundation models, classical computer vision, and hybrid approaches — optimizing for production deployment.

As a PhD researcher, I explore generative AI and 3D reconstruction. Published in IEEE journals on diffusion models and state space models. Completed peer reviews for IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Scientific Reports Nature.
